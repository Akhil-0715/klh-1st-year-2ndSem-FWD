COMPUTER SCIENCE DEPARTMENT

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) focuses on providing students with strong foundations in computing, programming, and problem-solving skills. It offers courses in areas such as data structures, algorithms, software engineering, artificial intelligence, and computer networks.


The CSE department also encourages innovation, teamwork, and continuous learning through workshops, seminars, coding competitions, and industry collaborations. Faculty members guide students in developing technical expertise and professional skills required to meet modern technological challenges.
